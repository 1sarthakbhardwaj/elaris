Creative Studio is the open, node-based canvas at the heart of ElarisLabs. Add nodes, wire them together, pick AI models, and iterate until your creative is ready to export, pack, or schedule — all in one place.

## How does the canvas work?

The canvas is an infinite workspace. Each **node** represents one AI operation. You chain nodes by connecting an upstream output to a downstream input — for example, piping a text-to-image output into an upscale node, then into a brand pack.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a node">
    Open the left toolbar or creation menu and choose Image, Video, Audio, or a utility node.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a mode and model">
    Select the operation mode (e.g. text-to-image vs. remove background) and pick an AI model or leave it on `auto`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect upstream outputs">
    Drag a connection from any upstream node's output port to feed reference media or source clips into the current node.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run and iterate">
    Generate. Results render on the node and become available as outputs for downstream steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save or export">
    Save the graph as a named **project**, export final media, or hand off to the Scheduler.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Projects and templates

| Concept | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Project** | A saved canvas — graph layout, model settings, and media references |
| **Template** | A published project other users can clone as a starting point |
| **Brand pack** | A multi-size export of one master creative |
| **Public view link** | A read-only share URL for stakeholder review |
| **Team share token** | A share token for collaborators inside your organization |

<Tip>
Browse **Templates** to clone proven workflows instead of starting from a blank canvas. Ad-oriented templates and Magic Layers live under [Ad Templates](/ad-templates/overview).
</Tip>
