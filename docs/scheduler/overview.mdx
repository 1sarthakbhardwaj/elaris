Scheduler is Elaris's publishing hub for organic social. Connect your accounts once, then create, schedule, and track posts to **Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, X, TikTok, Snapchat, and LinkedIn** from a single interface.

## What's inside Scheduler?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Calendar" icon="calendar" href="/scheduler/calendar-and-drafts">
    Visual timeline of every scheduled and published post. Drag to reschedule or click to edit.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Posts" icon="send" href="/scheduler/compose-and-schedule">
    All scheduled and published items for your connected accounts. Filter by platform or status.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Drafts" icon="file-pen" href="/scheduler/calendar-and-drafts">
    Save work-in-progress posts without committing to a publish time. Perfect for content awaiting approval.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Connections" icon="plug" href="/scheduler/connect-accounts">
    Link and manage social accounts. Each platform uses OAuth — tokens are stored securely per workspace.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Analytics" icon="chart-bar" href="/scheduler/analytics">
    Post-level performance metrics for everything you've published through Elaris.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Which platforms are supported?

Scheduler supports live publishing to all major social platforms:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Video & short-form">
    - **YouTube** — long-form video, Shorts
    - **TikTok** — short-form video
    - **Instagram** — Reels, Stories, feed posts
    - **Snapchat** — Stories
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Feed & community">
    - **Facebook** — Pages, feed posts, video
    - **X (Twitter)** — text, images, video
    - **LinkedIn** — personal profiles and Company Pages
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
Platform availability depends on which accounts you have connected and your workspace plan. Head to **Scheduler → Connections** to see what's active.
</Note>
