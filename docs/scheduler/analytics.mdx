**Scheduler → Analytics** shows you how your organic posts perform after they go live. Every post published through Elaris is tracked here — no manual export needed.

## What metrics are available?

Metrics are pulled directly from each platform's API. Availability varies by platform and account type:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Reach & impressions">
    - **Reach** — unique accounts that saw the post
    - **Impressions** — total times the post was displayed (including repeat views)

    Available on: Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Engagement">
    - **Likes / reactions** — platform-specific reaction counts
    - **Comments** — total comment count
    - **Shares / reposts** — amplification actions
    - **Saves** — bookmarks (Instagram, TikTok)
    - **Engagement rate** — computed as total engagements ÷ reach

    Available on: Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, X, LinkedIn, YouTube
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Video performance">
    - **Views** — number of times the video was played
    - **Watch time** — total minutes viewed (YouTube)
    - **Completion rate** — percentage of viewers who watched to the end

    Available on: YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
Some platforms (X, Snapchat) return a limited analytics set depending on your account tier. Fields unavailable from the platform API will appear as "—" in the Elaris UI.
</Note>

## How do I read the analytics view?

The Analytics view is organized by post. Each row shows:

1. **Post preview** — thumbnail and caption snippet
2. **Platform** — which connected account published it
3. **Published date** — exact timestamp
4. **Key metrics** — reach, engagement, and any platform-specific fields

Click any row to expand full per-platform metric details.

## What's the difference between Scheduler Analytics and Insights?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Scheduler Analytics — organic posts">
    Tracks performance for posts you published organically through the Scheduler. Best for measuring your content strategy and comparing posts over time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Insights — paid ad campaigns">
    Tracks performance across your paid ad spend on Meta, TikTok, and Snapchat. Includes creative-level scoring, cross-channel comparison, and predictive recommendations.

    Use [Insights](/insights/overview) when you need to evaluate ad ROI or optimize creative spend.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
Combine Scheduler Analytics with Insights for a full picture: use Scheduler Analytics to identify your top organic posts, then promote them as ads and track paid performance in Insights.
</Tip>
