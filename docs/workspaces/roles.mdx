---
title: Roles & permissions
description: Four workspace roles — Owner, Admin, Member, and Viewer — control who can bill, invite, generate, and read across your workspace and brands.
---

Elaris has four workspace roles. Permissions apply at the workspace level and can be further refined per brand grant.

## What can each role do?

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Owner">
    **Full control** over the workspace.

    - Manage billing, subscriptions, and payment methods
    - Add and remove members at any role level
    - Create, edit, and delete brands
    - Allocate and transfer credits
    - Delete the workspace
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Admin">
    **Operational control** without full billing access.

    - Invite and manage members (cannot promote to Owner)
    - Create and configure brands
    - Access most workspace settings
    - Billing access may be restricted depending on configuration
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Member">
    **Creation and generation** within granted brands.

    - Generate images, video, and audio in Studio
    - Run campaigns within assigned brands
    - Cannot manage members or billing
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Viewer">
    **Read-only** access by default.

    - View projects and assets
    - May see a banner to request a higher role
    - Generation may be restricted depending on brand configuration
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Why can't I generate content?

<Note>
  If you cannot generate, check **both** your workspace role and your brand membership. You need sufficient access at both levels. Viewers or members without brand access will see a permission banner — click it to request a higher role from an Admin or Owner.
</Note>

Exact permissions can vary by brand grants. Contact your workspace Owner or Admin to update your role.
