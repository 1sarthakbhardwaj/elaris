Elaris plans bundle seat access with plan-included credits. Upgrade or downgrade at any time from **Workspace → Plan tier**.

## Which plan is right for my team?

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Free">
    **Explore the product with limited credits.**

    - Access to core Studio features
    - Limited monthly generation credits
    - Ideal for individuals evaluating Elaris

    <Tip>Free plan credits reset each billing cycle. Purchased credit packs remain available even if you downgrade.</Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Basic">
    **~$49/mo — for growing teams.**

    - Higher monthly credit allocation
    - Multi-brand support
    - Priority generation queuing
    - Credit packs available for top-up
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Pro">
    **~$399/mo — for high-volume teams.**

    - Significantly higher monthly credits
    - Full collaboration features
    - Advanced brand controls and dedicated credit pools
    - API and MCP access included
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Enterprise">
    **Custom pricing — for large-scale, security-conscious deployments.**

    - Custom seat and credit volumes
    - SSO / SAML and advanced security controls

    [Contact sales →](mailto:sales@elarislabs.com)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  Exact entitlements for each tier are displayed on **Workspace → Plan tier** in the app. The figures above are indicative.
</Note>

## How does billing work?

Elaris uses **Stripe** for all payment processing.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upgrade your plan">
    Go to **Workspace → Plan tier** and click **Upgrade**. You'll be redirected to Stripe Checkout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage payment methods">
    Access the Stripe customer portal from **Payments** to update cards, download invoices, or cancel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Top up with credit packs">
    On eligible plans, purchase additional credit packs from the Credits section. Packs are added to your balance immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review payment history">
    All invoices and transactions appear under **Workspace → Payments**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
