**URL to Video** scrapes a product or landing page, extracts media, writes a script, generates a voiceover, and renders a finished video. It's the fastest path from a product URL to a publish-ready video.

<Warning>
URL to Video is a separate product from Campaign video mode and Studio video nodes. If you're unsure which to use, see the [Product map](/getting-started/product-map).
</Warning>

## How do I create a video from a URL?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Paste the URL">
    Provide the product page or landing page URL. The pipeline scrapes the page for images, video clips, copy, and product details automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review scraped media">
    Confirm which images and video clips the pipeline detected. Remove anything you don't want included in the final video.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure settings">
    Set duration, aspect ratio, language, and other render options to match your target platform and audience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine the script">
    Review the AI-generated script. Edit individual lines or regenerate sections to match your brand voice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate voiceover">
    Choose a voice from the available library and generate the speech track. Preview and regenerate specific lines if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview & render">
    Confirm the full preview, then start the render job. Rendering runs in the background — you can continue working elsewhere while it completes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download or publish">
    Export the finished file or send it to Scheduler for direct publishing to connected social accounts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How does URL to Video compare to other video tools?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="vs. Campaign video mode">
    **URL to Video** is URL-driven and focused on product storytelling with a voiceover. **Campaign video mode** is part of the multi-agent campaign wizard and works from a creative brief rather than a URL. Use URL to Video for quick, product-focused videos; use Campaign video mode when you need brand-strategy-grounded campaign creatives.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="vs. Studio video nodes">
    **Studio** is a freeform node-based editor for advanced compositing, animation, and multi-layer video production. URL to Video is a guided wizard — much faster for standard product videos, but less flexible for fully custom creative work.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
