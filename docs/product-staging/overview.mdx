**Product Staging** (`/product`) is a focused wizard for generating product-led stills fast. Paste a product URL or upload an image, generate staging concepts, and refine with conversational chat — all without running a full campaign.

## How does Product Staging work?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Provide your product">
    Paste a product URL or upload a product image. The pipeline extracts product details and prepares them for creative generation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate staging concepts">
    Review AI-generated staging concepts — different backgrounds, lighting scenarios, and compositions that showcase your product.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine with chat">
    Use the conversational chat interface to describe changes: swap the background, adjust the lighting, reposition the product, or shift the mood. Each message refines the current result.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview & save">
    Review the final outputs. Keep your winners and save them to your brand asset library for use in Scheduler or Studio.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How is Product Staging different from Campaigns or Studio?

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Product Staging">
    **Best for:** Fast, product-led stills with conversational refinement. No campaign brief required. Ideal when you have a product and need clean creative assets quickly.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Campaigns">
    **Best for:** Full multi-agent ad production — brand strategy, creative territories, 25–30 ideas, and platform-targeted variants. Use when you need a complete campaign, not just product shots.

    <Card title="Campaigns overview" icon="megaphone" href="/campaigns/overview">
      Learn how the multi-agent campaign pipeline works.
    </Card>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Studio">
    **Best for:** Freeform, node-based creative work — animations, advanced compositing, multi-layer video, and custom templates. Use when you need maximum creative control.

    <Card title="Studio overview" icon="layers" href="/studio/overview">
      Explore the full node-based creative editor.
    </Card>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
Use Product Staging to quickly generate hero product shots, then bring those assets into a **Campaign** (with-assets mode) or **Studio** for advanced creative production.
</Tip>
