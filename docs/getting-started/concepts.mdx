These concepts appear everywhere in ElarisLabs. Learn them once and the rest of the product clicks into place.

## What is a workspace?

A **workspace** is your team container. It owns the subscription plan, member roster, payment history, and the top-level **credit pool**. Brands live inside a workspace.

## What is a brand?

A **brand** holds identity (logos, colors, fonts, guidelines), campaigns, assets, brand-level credits, and members. Most generation is scoped to the selected brand. MCP API keys are **brand-bound**.

## How do credits work?

**Credits** meter AI usage. Pricing is anchored so that roughly **1 credit ≈ $0.008 of underlying model cost**. Credits can sit on the workspace pool and/or be allocated to brands. Studio, campaigns, URL-to-video, and paid MCP tools deduct credits.

  See [Credits](/workspaces/credits) for hierarchy, spend caps, and how deductions flow.
</Info>

## What is a Studio project?

A **project** is a saved Creative Studio canvas (nodes + connections). Projects can be shared via public slug or team token.

## What is a campaign?

A **campaign** is a guided multi-agent run: strategy → ideas → variants → prompts → images or video. Results land in the brand asset library.

## What is a node?

A **node** is a unit of work on the Creative Studio canvas — image, video, audio, captions, brand pack, layer editor, and more. Connect nodes to build workflows.

## What is a template?

A **template** is a reusable Studio (or ad) starting point. Browse published templates, clone into your canvas, or publish your own for the workspace.

## What is an integration?

An **integration** connects a social or ads account (Instagram, TikTok, Meta, YouTube, X, LinkedIn, Snapchat) for scheduling or insights. MCP and Slack are agent integrations that call the same tool surface.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do workspaces and brands relate?" icon="git-branch">
    One workspace can contain many brands. Billing and seats live at the workspace; creative work and most generation spend are scoped to a brand.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are MCP keys workspace-scoped or brand-scoped?" icon="key">
    Brand-scoped. Every brand-bound API key acts on one brand and bills that brand's credit balance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where do campaign outputs go?" icon="folder">
    Into the campaign results view and the brand asset library, so you can reuse them in Creative Studio or Scheduler.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
