ElarisLabs has several creative surfaces. Use this map to pick the right one.

| Surface | Best for | Entry |
|---------|----------|-------|
| **Creative Studio** | Open-ended image/video/audio workflows, editing, brand packs, Hyperframes | `/studio` |
| **Campaigns** | Full multi-agent ad sets from a brief | `/campaigns/new` |
| **URL to Video** | Product page → scripted voiceover video | `/url-to-video` |
| **Product Staging** | Chat-driven product creatives from a URL or upload | `/product` |
| **Ad Templates** | Layout-driven ads and Magic Layers | `/templates` + campaign ad-template mode |
| **Scheduler** | Calendar, drafts, social publishing | `/scheduler` |
| **Insights** | Paid social performance + recommendations | `/insights` |

## Which surface should I open?

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Explore freely" icon="palette">
    Use **Creative Studio** when you want maximum control: connect nodes, pick models, edit, pack, and export.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Full ad set" icon="megaphone">
    Use **Campaigns** when you have a brief and want strategy → ideas → variants → finished creatives.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Product page video" icon="film">
    Use **URL to Video** when you can paste a product URL and want a scripted voiceover clip without building a node graph.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Publish & measure" icon="chart-line">
    Use **Scheduler** to post and **Insights** to learn what performed — then feed winners back into Studio or Campaigns.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## What are the three ways to make video?

Teams often confuse these — they are different products:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Studio video nodes" icon="clapperboard">
    Generate or edit clips on the canvas: text-to-video, image-to-video, lip sync, motion control, Hyperframes animated pipelines, join/upscale, and more. Maximum creative control; you assemble the workflow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="URL to Video" icon="link">
    Paste a product URL. Elaris scrapes media, helps you pick a script and voiceover, then renders a finished video job. Optimized for product marketing clips, not freeform node graphs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Campaign video mode" icon="wand-sparkles">
    Inside the campaign wizard, choose the video mode to produce campaign video outputs as part of the multi-agent pipeline (alongside ideas and brand context).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  Studio video ≠ URL to Video ≠ Campaign video mode. Start from the surface that matches your goal, then move assets between them (for example Creative Studio → Scheduler).
</Warning>
