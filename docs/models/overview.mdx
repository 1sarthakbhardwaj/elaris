Elaris routes every generation through a curated model catalog spanning image, video, and audio. Credits scale with model tier, resolution, duration, and quality — all anchored to the same formula: **1 credit ≈ $0.008**.

## Which model should I use?

Use **`auto`** on image generation when you want Elaris's router to select a strong default. For fine-grained control, pick a specific model from the catalog pages below.

## How do credit costs scale?

| Factor | Effect on credit cost |
|--------|-----------------------|
| Model tier | Premium models cost more per generation |
| Resolution | 2K / 4K outputs multiply the base image cost |
| Duration | Video costs scale linearly with output seconds |
| Quality setting | Higher quality = higher token / compute cost |

<Tip>
  Studio always shows the estimated credit cost before you confirm a generation. You can switch models or adjust settings to fit your budget without losing your prompt or inputs.
</Tip>
